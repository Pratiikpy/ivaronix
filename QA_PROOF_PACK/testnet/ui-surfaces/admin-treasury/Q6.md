## What the UI gates (security observation)

The /admin/treasury route is wallet-gated for two reasons:
1. **State minimization** — anonymous visitors don't need to see the treasury balance
2. **Authority signaling** — the page only renders the action panel when the connected wallet IS the contract Ownable owner; non-owner wallets get a "not authorized" state (not captured here since no MM injection, but contract enforces it via `onlyOwner` modifier at SkillRunPayment.sol:244)

Both are correct defense-in-depth even though the on-chain `withdrawTreasury` already gates on `onlyOwner`. The UI gating is BEFORE the chain reverts; the chain gating is the final authority.
