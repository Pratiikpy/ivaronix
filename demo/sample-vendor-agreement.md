# Master Software Services Agreement

**Vendor:** Catalyst Analytics Inc. ("Vendor")
**Customer:** Acme Corp ("Customer")
**Effective date:** 1 June 2026
**Initial term:** 24 months

This Master Software Services Agreement ("Agreement") sets forth the terms under which Vendor will provide its analytics platform to Customer. By signing below, Customer agrees to the terms in this document and the attached Order Form.

---

## 1. Service description

Vendor will provide access to its hosted analytics platform ("Service") for use by up to 50 named users at Customer. The Service includes dashboards, reporting, and data-export functionality. Specific service-level commitments are described in Section 8 below.

---

## 2. Fees and payment

- **Annual fee.** Customer shall pay USD 180,000 per year, due in full within 14 days of invoice.
- **Payment terms.** All fees are non-refundable under any circumstance, including early termination, suspension, or change in Customer's business.
- **Price escalation.** Vendor may unilaterally increase fees by up to 12% per Renewal Term, with 30 days' written notice.
- **Late payment.** Past-due amounts accrue interest at 1.75% per month (approximately 23% APR) plus reasonable collection costs and legal fees.

---

## 3. Term and renewal

- **Initial term.** 24 months from the Effective date.
- **Automatic renewal.** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month terms unless Customer provides written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days before the end of the then-current term.
- **No termination for convenience.** Customer may not terminate this Agreement for convenience during the Initial term or any Renewal Term.

---

## 4. Liability and indemnification

- **Customer indemnification.** Customer shall indemnify, defend, and hold Vendor harmless against all claims, damages, and expenses (including attorneys' fees) arising from Customer's use of the Service, regardless of cause.
- **Vendor liability cap.** Vendor's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed three (3) months of fees paid by Customer.
- **No consequential damages.** In no event shall Vendor be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages, including lost profits or business interruption, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

---

## 5. Data and intellectual property

- **Customer data.** Customer grants Vendor a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, modify, distribute, and create derivative works of Customer data for any purpose Vendor deems appropriate, including training of Vendor's machine-learning models.
- **Aggregated data.** Vendor may use, distribute, and sell anonymized and aggregated data derived from Customer's use of the Service without restriction.
- **IP ownership.** All improvements, modifications, and derivative works of the Service, including those suggested or co-developed by Customer, remain the sole property of Vendor.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of non-public information disclosed under this Agreement for a period of three years from disclosure. The confidentiality obligation does not apply to information already in the receiving party's possession, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law.

---

## 7. Governing law and dispute resolution

- **Governing law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles.
- **Mandatory arbitration.** Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered by Vendor's chosen arbitration provider, in Delaware, conducted in English.
- **No jury trial.** Customer waives any right to a jury trial.
- **No class action.** Customer waives any right to participate in a class action against Vendor.

---

## 8. Service levels

- **Uptime target.** Vendor will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain 99.5% monthly uptime.
- **Remedies.** Customer's sole remedy for any uptime failure is a service credit of up to 5% of the prior month's fees, applied to a future invoice. Service credits are non-refundable.
- **Maintenance windows.** Vendor may take the Service offline for scheduled or emergency maintenance without notice and such time does not count against the uptime target.

---

## 9. Miscellaneous

- **Assignment.** Customer may not assign this Agreement without Vendor's prior written consent. Vendor may assign at any time without notice.
- **Entire agreement.** This Agreement, together with the attached Order Form, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
- **Amendment.** Vendor may amend this Agreement at any time by posting an updated version to Vendor's website. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance.
